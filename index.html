📖 Help & Documentation

→ Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Financial Command Centre! This app helps you track your complete financial picture including crypto, equities, and debt.

1. Add Your Assets

Go to Portfolio → Crypto or Equities and click "+ Add" to enter your holdings

2. Track Your Debts

Add credit cards and loans in Portfolio → Liabilities to optimize your payoff strategy

3. Set Your Goals

Create financial goals in Planning → Goals to track your progress over time

4. Use the AI Assistant

Ask questions like "What's my net worth?" or "When will I be debt free?"
