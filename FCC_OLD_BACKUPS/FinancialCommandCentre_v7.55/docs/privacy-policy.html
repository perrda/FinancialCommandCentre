Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Financial Command Centre (FCC). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Financial Data

FCC processes the following financial information that you provide:

  • Cryptocurrency holdings and wallet addresses
  • Stock holdings and portfolio positions
  • Asset valuations and price data
  • Liability information (loans, debts)
  • Net worth calculations and financial goals

Important: All financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your financial data to our servers.

2.2 Third-Party API Calls

FCC integrates with the following external services to fetch real-time pricing data only:

  • CoinGecko API - Cryptocurrency price data
  • Finnhub API - Stock price and market data
  • ExchangeRate-API - Currency conversion rates

These services receive only API requests for market data and do not receive your financial holdings or personal information.

2.3 Cloud Sync (Optional)

FCC offers optional Firebase-based cloud synchronization for cross-device access. This feature is:

  • Completely optional and disabled by default
  • Requires explicit user authentication and opt-in
  • User-controlled - you can disable sync at any time
  • End-to-end encrypted before transmission

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Display real-time portfolio valuations and calculations
  • Provide price updates from third-party market data APIs
  • Generate financial reports and analytics
  • Synchronize data across your devices (if opted in)
  • Improve application functionality and user experience
  • Maintain and support the application

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all your financial data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage and IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

4.2 Cloud Storage

If you enable cloud synchronization:

  • Data is encrypted before transmission
  • Firebase provides secure cloud storage
  • Only you can access your encrypted data with your login credentials
  • You can delete cloud data at any time

4.3 Security Measures

FCC implements industry-standard security practices including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all API communications
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Data validation and sanitization
  • Regular security updates

5. Third-Party Services

FCC integrates with the following third parties:

  • CoinGecko - Privacy Policy
  • Finnhub - Privacy Policy
  • ExchangeRate-API - Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google) - Privacy Policy

We recommend reviewing their privacy policies to understand how they handle data.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We want to be absolutely clear: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or financial data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

We only share data when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protecting the safety and security of our users
  • With your explicit consent

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you manually delete it or clear application storage
  • Cloud Data: Retained as long as your account is active. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days
  • API Logs: Third-party services may retain logs per their privacy policies

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

8.1 GDPR (European Users)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

8.2 CCPA (California Users)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: support@financialcommandcentre.app

9. Children's Privacy

FCC is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information and terminate the child's account.

10. International Users

If you are accessing FCC from outside your country of residence, please be aware that your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your home country.

11. Cookies and Tracking

FCC minimally uses cookies and tracking technologies. We use:

  • Session cookies for authentication (only if cloud sync enabled)
  • Local storage for application preferences (on your device only)
  • No third-party advertising or analytics tracking
